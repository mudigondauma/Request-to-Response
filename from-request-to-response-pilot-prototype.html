                      Pilot review model

                      This is the minimal review logic needed to de-risk the thin slice before any scale conversation.
                      • Green
                        Low sensitivity, complete evidence, internal-only use. Auto-cleared for internal draft circulation, still logged.
                      • Amber
                        Material impact or incomplete evidence. Named reviewer must approve or return the draft.
                      • Red
                        Restricted data, compliance conflict, or unsupported claims. Hold and escalate before any use.
                      Prototype review thresholds
                      • Restricted / regulated sensitivity automatically triggers Red.
                      • Missing critical fields or incomplete source coverage trigger Amber.
                      • Open questions on pricing, legal, or compliance claims suppress Green clearance.
                      • Every override is logged to support pilot learning and governance evidence.

                      Architecture and controls

                      A deliberately narrow, governable architecture that matches the use case you documented: multi-agent reasoning under orchestration, with human accountability preserved.

                      1. Intake

                      Teams submit a shaping request with problem context, approved sources, constraints, and desired output.

                      2. Opportunity Interpretation Agent

                      Classifies the request, infers likely buying priorities, and identifies gaps that affect shaping quality.

                      3. Solution Pattern Agent

                      Retrieves reusable architectures, propositions, delivery models, and precedent cases from approved assets.

                      4. Risk Agent

                      Surfaces commercial, delivery, and dependency risks; flags missing assumptions and feasibility concerns.

                      5. Governance Agent

                      Assesses sensitivity, approval requirements, and policy concerns before any internal circulation.

                      6. Executive Briefing Agent

                      Synthesises the pack into a concise internal narrative for deal leaders and executive stakeholders.

                      7. Accountable human review

                      Approves, revises, or escalates. The human remains the decision-maker for material outputs.

                      Core guardrails

                      • Read-only access in pilot mode
                      • Draft-only operation with no autonomous external send
                      • Role-based access tied to sensitivity
                      • Approved-source retrieval and source traceability
                      • Fallback to manual review when evidence is weak

                      Human review design

                      • Green / Amber / Red routing
                      • Confidence labels and inference tags
                      • Reviewer actions logged as evidence
                      • No binding commercial or compliance claims without approval
                      • Escalation for restricted data or ambiguous policy concerns

                      Production path after the pilot

                      • Connect Teams / Copilot as the front door
                      • Integrate SharePoint, proposal libraries, CRM context, and delivery playbooks
                      • Add authentication, permissions, and model telemetry
                      • Expand opportunity categories only after KPI stability is proven

                      What this prototype intentionally leaves out

                      • Live LLM calls
                      • CRM or workflow write-back
                      • Pricing engine integration
                      • Legal sign-off automation
                      • Customer-facing submission
